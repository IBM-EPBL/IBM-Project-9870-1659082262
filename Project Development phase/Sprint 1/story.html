PLASMA DONOR APPLICATION

WELCOME

Patients all over the world rely on plasma protein therapies to treat rare, chronic diseases. These individuals rely on the generousity and commitment of plasma donors, You may donate plasma in one of more than 900 licensed and certified plasma collection centers located in US and Europe. Plasma often is reffered to as the "gift of life" because it is essential starting material needed to manufacture therapies that help thousands of people worldwide with rare, chronic diseases to live healthier, productive and fulfilling lives.
